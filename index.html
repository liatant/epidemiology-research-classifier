Descriptive

Prevalence, incidence, burden, rates, distributions, surveillance, trends, and population characterization.

Analytic

Associations, exposure-outcome relationships, causal effects, risk factors, adjusted estimates, and hypothesis testing.

Predictive

Forecasting, risk prediction, classification, model development, validation, calibration, AUC, and prediction scores.

Prescriptive

Recommendations, decision support, treatment or intervention selection, policy optimization, and action rules.
